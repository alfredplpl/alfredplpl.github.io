Projects

I research Human-Robot Interaction and Computer Vision, Robot Vision. Especially, I focus on Theory of mind. My goal is to develop the robot that can recognize human internal states such as intents, desires, beliefs. The robot can also interact with humans in the real world. To achieve the goal, currently I decide the approach that integrates machine learning applications such as wimage recognition, voice recognition, voice synthesis and so on.
